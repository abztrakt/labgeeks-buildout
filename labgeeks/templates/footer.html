//Customize

If you want to customize your own footer, add a line in TEMPLATE_DIRS in local_settings.py that points to where you want your server to look for the template. For example, I put another footer.html in a new folder I created in templates/ called internal/. Therefore, I will add the line "path/to/directory/.../templates/internal" in TEMPLATE_DIRS before the other paths so that this footer.html will come first.
